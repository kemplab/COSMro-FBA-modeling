Summaries for SLC28A3 Gene

Entrez Gene Summary for SLC28A3 Gene

  • Nucleoside transporters, such as SLC28A3, regulate multiple cellular processes, including neurotransmission, vascular tone, adenosine concentration in the vicinity of cell surface receptors, and transport and metabolism of nucleoside drugs. SLC28A3 shows broad specificity for pyrimidine and purine nucleosides (Ritzel et al., 2001 [PubMed 11032837]).[supplied by OMIM, Mar 2008]

GeneCards Summary for SLC28A3 Gene

SLC28A3 (Solute Carrier Family 28 Member 3) is a Protein Coding gene. Among its related pathways are Gemcitabine, Pharmacokinetics/Pharmacodynamics Pathway and One carbon pool by folate. GO annotations related to this gene include nucleoside binding and pyrimidine- and adenine-specific:sodium symporter activity. An important paralog of this gene is SLC28A2.

UniProtKB/Swiss-Prot for SLC28A3 Gene

  • S28A3_HUMAN,Q9HAS3

  • Sodium-dependent, pyrimidine- and purine-selective. Involved in the homeostasis of endogenous nucleosides. Exhibits the transport characteristics of the nucleoside transport system cib or N3 subtype (N3/cib) (with marked transport of both thymidine and inosine). Employs a 2:1 sodium/nucleoside ratio. Also able to transport gemcitabine, 3-azido-3-deoxythymidine (AZT), ribavirin and 3-deazauridine.
