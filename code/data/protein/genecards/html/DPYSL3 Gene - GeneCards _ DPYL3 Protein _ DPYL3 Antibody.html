Summaries for DPYSL3 Gene

GeneCards Summary for DPYSL3 Gene

DPYSL3 (Dihydropyrimidinase Like 3) is a Protein Coding gene. Diseases associated with DPYSL3 include subependymal glioma. Among its related pathways are Developmental Biology and Semaphorin interactions. GO annotations related to this gene include hydrolase activity and phosphoprotein binding. An important paralog of this gene is CRMP1.

UniProtKB/Swiss-Prot for DPYSL3 Gene

  • DPYL3_HUMAN,Q14195

  • Necessary for signaling by class 3 semaphorins and subsequent remodeling of the cytoskeleton. Plays a role in axon guidance, neuronal growth cone collapse and cell migration (By similarity).
