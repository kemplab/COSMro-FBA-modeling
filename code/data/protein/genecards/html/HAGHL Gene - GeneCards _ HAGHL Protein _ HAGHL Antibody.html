Summaries for HAGHL Gene

GeneCards Summary for HAGHL Gene

HAGHL (Hydroxyacylglutathione Hydrolase-Like) is a Protein Coding gene. Among its related pathways are Pyruvate metabolism. GO annotations related to this gene include hydrolase activity and hydroxyacylglutathione hydrolase activity. An important paralog of this gene is HAGH.

UniProtKB/Swiss-Prot for HAGHL Gene

  • HAGHL_HUMAN,Q6PII5

  • Hydrolase acting on ester bonds.
