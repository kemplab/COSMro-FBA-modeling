Summaries for LAP3 Gene

GeneCards Summary for LAP3 Gene

LAP3 (Leucine Aminopeptidase 3) is a Protein Coding gene. Diseases associated with LAP3 include cercarial dermatitis. Among its related pathways are Arginine and proline metabolism and Glutathione metabolism. GO annotations related to this gene include peptidase activity and aminopeptidase activity. An important paralog of this gene is NPEPL1.

UniProtKB/Swiss-Prot for LAP3 Gene

  • AMPL_HUMAN,P28838

  • Presumably involved in the processing and regular turnover of intracellular proteins. Catalyzes the removal of unsubstituted N-terminal amino acids from various peptides.
