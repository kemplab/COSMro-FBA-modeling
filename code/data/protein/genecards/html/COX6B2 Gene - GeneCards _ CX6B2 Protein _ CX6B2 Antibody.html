Summaries for COX6B2 Gene

GeneCards Summary for COX6B2 Gene

COX6B2 (Cytochrome C Oxidase Subunit 6B2) is a Protein Coding gene. Among its related pathways are Alzheimer's disease and Cardiac muscle contraction. GO annotations related to this gene include cytochrome-c oxidase activity. An important paralog of this gene is COX6B1.

UniProtKB/Swiss-Prot for COX6B2 Gene

  • CX6B2_HUMAN,Q6YFQ2

  • Connects the two COX monomers into the physiological dimeric form.
