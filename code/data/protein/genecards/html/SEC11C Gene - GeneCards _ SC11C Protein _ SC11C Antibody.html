Summaries for SEC11C Gene

GeneCards Summary for SEC11C Gene

SEC11C (SEC11 Homolog C, Signal Peptidase Complex Subunit) is a Protein Coding gene. Among its related pathways are Protein export and Gene Expression. GO annotations related to this gene include peptidase activity and serine-type peptidase activity. An important paralog of this gene is SEC11A.

UniProtKB/Swiss-Prot for SEC11C Gene

  • SC11C_HUMAN,Q9BY50

  • Component of the microsomal signal peptidase complex which removes signal peptides from nascent proteins as they are translocated into the lumen of the endoplasmic reticulum.
