Summaries for RDH14 Gene

GeneCards Summary for RDH14 Gene

RDH14 (Retinol Dehydrogenase 14 (All-Trans/9-Cis/11-Cis)) is a Protein Coding gene. Among its related pathways are Visual Cycle in Retinal Rods and Signaling by Retinoic Acid. GO annotations related to this gene include oxidoreductase activity and coenzyme binding. An important paralog of this gene is RDH12.

UniProtKB/Swiss-Prot for RDH14 Gene

  • RDH14_HUMAN,Q9HBH5

  • Exhibits an oxidoreductive catalytic activity towards retinoids. Most efficient as an NADPH-dependent retinal reductase. Displays high activity toward 9-cis and all-trans-retinol. No steroid dehydrogenase activity detected.
