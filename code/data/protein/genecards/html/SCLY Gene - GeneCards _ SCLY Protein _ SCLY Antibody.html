Summaries for SCLY Gene

Entrez Gene Summary for SCLY Gene

  • Selenocysteine lyase (SCLY; EC 4.4.1.16) catalyzes the pyridoxal 5-prime phosphate-dependent conversion of L-selenocysteine to L-alanine and elemental selenium (Mihara et al., 2000 [PubMed 10692412]).[supplied by OMIM, Mar 2008]

GeneCards Summary for SCLY Gene

SCLY (Selenocysteine Lyase) is a Protein Coding gene. Diseases associated with SCLY include patellofemoral pain syndrome and multiple personality disorder. Among its related pathways are Metabolism and Selenocompound metabolism. GO annotations related to this gene include transferase activity and lyase activity. An important paralog of this gene is ENSG00000272897.
