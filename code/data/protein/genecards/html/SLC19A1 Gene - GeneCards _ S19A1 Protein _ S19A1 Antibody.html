Summaries for SLC19A1 Gene

Entrez Gene Summary for SLC19A1 Gene

  • The membrane protein encoded by this gene is a transporter of folate and is involved in the regulation of intracellular concentrations of folate. Three transcript variants encoding different isoforms have been found for this gene.[provided by RefSeq, Mar 2011]

GeneCards Summary for SLC19A1 Gene

SLC19A1 (Solute Carrier Family 19 Member 1) is a Protein Coding gene. Diseases associated with SLC19A1 include placental choriocarcinoma and methotrexate toxicity or dose selection. Among its related pathways are Vitamin digestion and absorption and Methotrexate Pathway, Pharmacokinetics. GO annotations related to this gene include oxidoreductase activity and folic acid transporter activity. An important paralog of this gene is SLC19A2.

UniProtKB/Swiss-Prot for SLC19A1 Gene

  • S19A1_HUMAN,P41440

  • Transporter for the intake of folate. Uptake of folate in human placental choriocarcinoma cells occurs by a novel mechanism called potocytosis which functionally couples three components, namely the folate receptor, the folate transporter, and a V-type H(+)-pump.
