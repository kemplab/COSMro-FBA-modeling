Summaries for ACSS3 Gene

GeneCards Summary for ACSS3 Gene

ACSS3 (Acyl-CoA Synthetase Short-Chain Family Member 3) is a Protein Coding gene. Among its related pathways are Metabolism and Propanoate metabolism. GO annotations related to this gene include acetate-CoA ligase activity. An important paralog of this gene is AACS.

UniProtKB/Swiss-Prot for ACSS3 Gene

  • ACSS3_HUMAN,Q9H6R3

  • Activates acetate so that it can be used for lipid synthesis or for energy generation.
