Summaries for MTHFD2L Gene

GeneCards Summary for MTHFD2L Gene

MTHFD2L (Methylenetetrahydrofolate Dehydrogenase (NADP+ Dependent) 2-Like) is a Protein Coding gene. Among its related pathways are Metabolism and One carbon pool by folate. GO annotations related to this gene include formate-tetrahydrofolate ligase activity and methylenetetrahydrofolate dehydrogenase (NADP+) activity. An important paralog of this gene is MTHFD2.
