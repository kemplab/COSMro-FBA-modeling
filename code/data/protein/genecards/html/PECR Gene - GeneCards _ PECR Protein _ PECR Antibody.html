Summaries for PECR Gene

GeneCards Summary for PECR Gene

PECR (Peroxisomal Trans-2-Enoyl-CoA Reductase) is a Protein Coding gene. Among its related pathways are Fatty Acyl-CoA Biosynthesis and Fatty acid metabolism. GO annotations related to this gene include receptor binding and coenzyme binding. An important paralog of this gene is DECR2.

UniProtKB/Swiss-Prot for PECR Gene

  • PECR_HUMAN,Q9BY49

  • Participates in chain elongation of fatty acids. Has no 2,4-dienoyl-CoA reductase activity.
